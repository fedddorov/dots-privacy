Privacy Policy

Dots Brain Speed Training Game — last updated June 19, 2026

Dots does not collect any data.

Data collection

Dots does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. It makes no network connections.

On-device data

Your settings and your local leaderboard (scores, levels, times) are stored only on your device and never leave it. Deleting the app removes this data.

Children

Dots is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.
